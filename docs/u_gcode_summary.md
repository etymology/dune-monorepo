BtoF(b_pin) converts B pin to the opposite F pin.

On wrap 1
B1200+wrap on the -y side increments by +1
B2002-wrap on the -x side increments by -1
BtoF(B2002-wrap) on the +x side
BtoF(B400+wrap) on the -x side
B400+wrap on the +x side
B401-wrap on the +y side
BtoF(B401-wrap) on the -y side
BtoF(2001+wrap) on the +x side
B2001+wrap on the -x side
B1201-wrap on the +x side
BtoF(B1201-wrap) on the -x side
BtoF(1201+wrap) on the +y side
B1201+wrap on the -y side
